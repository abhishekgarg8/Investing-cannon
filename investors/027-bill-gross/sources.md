## Search limitations and cautions

- PIMCO Total Return performance should be reconstructed from primary annual reports and raw total-return data in later tasks; this profile uses the best public academic summary found during the run.
- AUM figures refer to different scopes: Total Return Fund AUM, PIMCO total firm AUM, PIMCO third-party AUM, and later/current PIMCO AUM. Do not combine them without labels.
- Foundation asset and donation figures should be verified against Form 990s in later tasks.
