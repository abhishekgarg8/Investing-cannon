# Jim Rogers - Task G Source Map

## Task G source map - mental models (2026-06-29)

1. [Jack Schwager, *Market Wizards* PDF mirror](https://www.valueplays.net/wp-content/uploads/41775536-Market-Wizards.pdf) - Core source for Rogers's do-nothing discipline, catalyst rule, German equities process example, early shorting/path-risk lessons, and concentrated-opportunity psychology; still needs authorized-edition page check.
2. [JimRogers.com - More about Jim](https://www.jimrogers.com/contact-morejim/) - Official source for travel as field research and country-observation method.
3. [Penguin Random House - *Investment Biker*](https://www.penguinrandomhouse.com/books/156700/investment-biker-by-jim-rogers/) - Publisher source for the motorcycle journey and investment-travel framing.
4. [Penguin Random House - *Adventure Capitalist*](https://www.penguinrandomhouse.com/books/156697/adventure-capitalist-by-jim-rogers/) - Publisher source for the 116-country car journey and field-research corpus.
5. [RICI Handbook, January 28, 2026 PDF](https://www.beelandinterests.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/02/RICI-Handbook_JAN.28.2026.pdf) - Current primary methodology for RICI's broad commodity-futures basket, sub-index weights, exchanges, currencies, and calculation/roll rules.
6. [SEC FWP - RICI Total Return ELEMENTS ETNs, 2007](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/352960/000110465907073158/a07-24898_5fwp.htm) - Primary product-risk source for RICI-linked ETNs, including no principal protection, issuer credit risk, fees, and commodity volatility.
7. [SEC S-1/A - Rogers International Raw Materials Fund, 2010](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1118384/000119312510213098/ds1a.htm) - Primary source for commodity-pool mechanics, long-only RICI replication, fees, Refco/legal context, and the 59.81% peak-to-valley drawdown.
8. [RBS RICI Enhanced ETN pricing supplement, 2012](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/729153/000095010312005697/dp33796_424b5-rici.htm) - Primary source for enhanced-index committee/calc-agent conflicts, roll-methodology variation, and product-holder conflict warnings.
9. [Beeland Interests - Legal Notices](https://www.beelandinterests.com/legal-notices/) - Current official legal/disclaimer source for benchmark-regulation perimeter, ownership, and data/error disclaimers.
10. [CFA Institute - "Jim Rogers: Bullish on China, Commodities, and Agriculture"](https://blogs.cfainstitute.org/blog/2011/05/12/jim-rogers-bullish-on-china-commodities-and-agriculture/) - Strong near-primary event write-up for hard-assets, money-printing, commodities, and agriculture themes.
11. [S&P Global - Insight Conversation with Jim Rogers, 2020](https://www.spglobal.com/energy/en/news-research/blog/crude-oil/052020-insight-conversation-jim-rogers-beeland-interests) - Direct interview source for supply-demand emphasis, RICI framing, and commodity-cycle process.
12. [S&P Global - Insight Conversation with Jim Rogers, 2021](https://www.spglobal.com/energy/en/news-research/blog/crude-oil/083121-ic-jim-rogers-quantum-beeland) - Direct interview source for agriculture opportunity, farmer demographics, China demand, and energy-transition metals.
13. [Morningstar India - learning from mistakes and loving critics](https://www.morningstar.in/posts/76296/jim-rogers-on-learning-from-mistakes-and-loving-his-critics.aspx) - Direct interview source for mistake attribution, market-action warning, and post-entry humility.
14. [Wealthion - "I sold almost all my stocks"](https://wealthion.com/news/jim-rogers-i-sold-almost-all-my-stocks-ive-seen-this-party-before) - 2025 transcript/article source for cash-heavy late-cycle posture, gold/silver, debt warnings, and free-trade/tariff concerns.
15. [Anadolu Agency - 2026 major correction interview](https://www.aa.com.tr/en/economy/american-investor-jim-rogers-warns-of-major-correction-in-us-global-markets/3792180) - Current interview for sold-U.S.-stocks statement, remaining exposures, and correction-warning framing.
16. [Economic Times - 2026 India/gold/silver interview](https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/markets/expert-view/jim-rogers-praises-india-us-trade-deal-eyes-long-term-gains-in-gold-silver/articleshow/127884415.cms?from=mdr) - Current source for price discipline, India policy shift, and gold/silver structural view.
17. [NBER - Gorton and Rouwenhorst, "Facts and Fantasies about Commodity Futures"](https://www.nber.org/system/files/working_papers/w10595/w10595.pdf) - Independent academic context on commodity futures as an asset class, inflation correlation, and diversification; not used as proof of Rogers's timing.
18. [Illinois Appellate Court opinion, 2014 IL App (1st) 13-3952](https://www.illinoiscourts.gov/files/1133952_R23.pdf/opinion) - Court record for Beeland/Refco-related litigation perimeter and governance/counterparty-risk caveats.
19. [SEC/Waton Financial 424B4, 2025](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1987363/000114036125011710/ny20017183x17_424b4.htm) - Current public-filing source for Rogers's March 2025 director/senior-adviser role and continuing Beeland/RICI affiliation.
20. [JimRogers.com - 29 June 2026 WhatsApp fraud warning](https://www.jimrogers.com/whatsapp-investor-group-fraud-warning/) - Current official authentication warning; essential source for quote/recommendation hygiene and social-media impersonation risk.
