### Task A search notes and caveats

- Searches covered Robertson/Tiger biographies, Robertson Foundation and family-foundation records, UNC/Duke/Robertson Scholars sources, Tiger closing reports, Tiger's 2000 investor letter, SEC 13F and IAPD/ADV records, legal/tax disputes, Tiger Cubs/seeding, current/posthumous Tiger Management developments, and criticism/legal terms.
- The best return evidence is triangulated but not fully audited: Washington Post gives contemporaneous 2000 reporting, Institutional Investor gives a 2010 full-period 25% figure, and the TurtleTrader page mirrors Robertson's own 2000 letter. No original partnership statements, audited monthly returns, or investor letters from an official Tiger archive were found.
- The 31.7%-32% record refers to the run through August 1998, not the full 1980-2000 outside-capital life after the final drawdown. The profile explicitly keeps that distinction.
- Later Tiger Management L.L.C. 13F filings and Tiger Management Advisors regulatory records are not comparable to original Tiger hedge-fund AUM or returns.
- Mandatory legal/criticism searches found the 2010 New York City statutory-residency tax case and 2024 posthumous Tiger/Hound litigation. No opened source showed an SEC enforcement action against Robertson tied to Tiger's investment management.
- Individual Tiger Cub names and performance claims should be verified in later tasks; this profile uses the network mainly as institutional legacy, not as a sourced genealogy.
