# Bill Ackman - Sources

Task A profile source map. Sources are ranked by usefulness for building the first-pass biography, vehicle map, track record, and legal/criticism context.

## Tier 1 - Primary and issuer / regulator sources

1. [Pershing Square Holdings May 2026 fact sheet](https://pershingsquareholdings.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/06/PSH-Fact-Sheet-May-2026-Website.pdf) - Best compact current snapshot of PSH structure, inception date, portfolio policy, May 31 2026 NAV/AUM, holdings, fee terms, and PSH standalone performance.
2. [PSCM Annual Investor Presentation, February 2026](https://assets.pershingsquareholdings.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/11144917/2026-Annual-Investor-Presentation.pdf) - Main source for Pershing's long-run composite PSLP-to-PSH return series, annual returns, 2025 portfolio review, and the "permanent capital era" framing. Important caveat: the long-run return assumes PSLP investment converted into PSH, not one continuous public vehicle.
3. [PSCM Form ADV Part 2A/2B, March 2026](https://pershingsquareholdings.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/Form-ADV-Part-2A-and-Part-2B-Dated-March-2026.pdf) - Primary regulatory brochure for PSCM fees, regulatory AUM, total AUM including HHH, adviser structure, personnel, and conflicts.
4. [Pershing Square USA N-2 registration statement, 2026](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/2002660/000114036126008532/ny20064799x1_n2.htm) - Primary SEC source for PSUS, the three core funds, employee/affiliate alignment, management-company structure, and Ackman's role as sole portfolio manager with ultimate decision authority.
5. [Pershing Square Inc. S-1, 2026](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/2026053/000114036126008560/ny20040230x14_s1.htm) - Primary source for the management-company IPO architecture, PS Inc. / Holdco conversion, and public management-company risk factors.
6. [Pershing Square Inc. EX-99.1, June 2026](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/2026053/000119312526257657/ps-ex99_1.htm) - Useful AUM roll-forward and April 30 2026 AUM source, including PSUS and HHH treatment.
7. [Pershing Square Holdings website](https://pershingsquareholdings.com/) - Current PSH public-company homepage with listing, strategy, live NAV/date context, materials index, and press releases.
8. [Pershing Square Philanthropies - Bill Ackman biography](https://pershingsquarephilanthropies.org/people/bill-ackman) - Official biography for Pershing founding, Gotham background, philanthropic commitments, and style summary.
9. [Pershing Square SPARC - William Ackman bio](https://pershingsquaresparcholdings.com/team/william-ackman/) - Official biography for education, Gotham, Ackman Brothers & Singer, HHH executive chairman status, and SPARC/PSTH roles.
10. [HHH May 5 2025 Pershing Square transaction release](https://investor.howardhughes.com/news-releases/news-release-details/pershing-square-invest-900-million-acquire-nine-million-newly) - Primary issuer source for the $900 million HHH investment, 46.9% ownership, voting cap, and strategic transformation plan.
11. [HHH 2025 Form 10-K](https://investor.howardhughes.com/static-files/a52456a6-684f-4228-a654-8835a7dd1254) - Primary HHH risk-factor source on Pershing's influence, Ackman as executive chairman, Ryan Israel as CIO, and potential misalignment with other shareholders.
12. [PSH June 4 2026 HHH preferred-stock completion release](https://pershingsquareholdings.com/pershing-square-holdings-ltd-announces-completion-of-investment-in-howard-hughes-holdings-inc-preferred-stock/) - Primary PSH source for the $1 billion HHH preferred-stock subscription tied to Vantage.
13. [FTC Herbalife settlement press release, 2016](https://www.ftc.gov/news-events/news/press-releases/2016/07/herbalife-will-restructure-its-multi-level-marketing-operations-pay-200-million-consumer-redress) - Primary regulator source for Herbalife's $200 million judgment and business-model restructuring.
14. [FTC Herbalife refunds page](https://www.ftc.gov/enforcement/refunds/herbalife-refunds) - Regulator summary of the consumer-redress program and allegations that Herbalife deceived consumers about earnings potential.
15. [Allergan SEC exhibit: complaint announcement, 2014](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/850693/000119312514291051/d769282dex99a28.htm) - Primary contemporaneous source for Allergan's federal-law allegations against Valeant, Pershing Square, and Ackman.
16. [Pershing Square Allergan settlement announcement, 2017](https://pershingsquareholdings.com/https-pershingsquareholdings-com-pershing-square-announces-agreement-in-principle-to-settle-lawsuits-related-to-allergan/) - Primary Pershing-side settlement source, including the $290 million total, Pershing's $193.75 million share, and Ackman's denial of merit.
17. [Ohio Attorney General Allergan settlement release, 2017](https://www.ohioattorneygeneral.gov/Media/News-Releases/December-2017/Lawsuit-Settlement-on-Insider-Trading-Case) - Public-plaintiff source for the settlement and allegations in the Allergan insider-trading litigation.
18. [Ackman Senate Special Committee on Aging testimony, 2016](https://www.aging.senate.gov/imo/media/doc/SCA_Ackman_4_27_16.pdf) - Primary own-words source on Valeant, Pershing's role, and 2016 capital under management; useful for later own-words and mistakes tasks.
19. [PSTH SEC exhibit on terminating UMG transaction, 2021](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1811882/000119312521218758/d199415dex991.htm) - Primary source for why PSTH did not proceed with the UMG transaction.
20. [Pershing Square Tontine July 2022 shareholder letter](https://pershingsquareholdings.com/https-pershingsquareholdings-com-p10529/) - Primary source for the return of $4 billion of PSTH trust capital.
21. [SPARC SEC exhibit, September 2023](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1895582/000119312523247567/d553088dex991.htm) - Primary source for SPARC effectiveness and SPAR distribution to former PSTH holders.

## Tier 2 - Strong secondary / criticism / legal context

22. [The New Yorker - "Financiers Fight Over the American Dream," 2017](https://www.newyorker.com/magazine/2017/03/06/financiers-fight-over-the-american-dream) - Deep narrative reconstruction of the Herbalife campaign, including Pershing's research apparatus, public presentation, lobbying, Icahn/Loeb counter-pressure, and the FTC outcome.
23. [Vanity Fair - "Is Bill Ackman Toast?", 2016](https://www.vanityfair.com/news/2016/10/is-bill-ackman-toast) - Critical profile of the Valeant/Herbalife drawdown, concentration risk, AUM shrinkage, and opposing hedge-fund views. Use carefully as a narrative source, not as audited data.
24. [AIC / Bloomberg summary of HHH lawsuit, 2026](https://www.theaic.co.uk/aic/news/industry-news/morning-briefing-howard-hughes-shareholder-sues-pershing-square-capital) - Secondary current legal context for the February 2026 HHH shareholder lawsuit; should be replaced or supplemented with docket materials later.
25. [Propmodo summary of HHH lawsuit, 2026](https://propmodo.com/pershing-square-faces-lawsuit-over-howard-hughes-control-deal/) - Secondary explainer on the HHH control-deal lawsuit and minority-shareholder control-premium allegations; useful lead, not a substitute for court filings.
